Our counsellors will support you whilst you to decide what to do next, but whatever you decide it is important that you are aware of and take special care of your own health and wellbeing. Be aware that counselling and support services are available to you to help you come to terms with or address any personal issues resulting from what has happened to you. No matter what you decide you must remind yourself that sexual assault is a crime and you should never blame yourself! In bSafe there are two ways you can report the crime to police.You can report the crime personally or via your near ones. Public can also respond to such incidents through this portal. We can provide information about what happened to the police – anonymously.
